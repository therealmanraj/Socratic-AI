About Socratic AI Tutor

Socratic AI Tutor helps students develop critical thinking skills by engaging them in interactive, step-by-step conversations powered by AI. No more copying answers — students learn through guided questioning.

Key Features

Guided Learning

AI asks leading questions, encouraging students to think critically.

Critical Thinking Score

Tracks student reliance on hints vs independent answers.

Teacher Dashboard

Full visibility into student learning progress.

Curriculum Customization

Schools can upload materials directly into the AI’s knowledge base.

How It Works

  • Student asks a question.
  • AI responds with a leading question, not a direct answer.
  • Hints provided only if needed, reducing over-reliance.
  • Critical Thinking Score tracks independent effort.
  • Teachers access conversation history and student data.
